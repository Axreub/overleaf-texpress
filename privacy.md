# Privacy Policy — Overleaf TeXpress
Last updated: April 25, 2026

## Overview
Overleaf TeXpress is a browser extension that expands LaTeX shortcuts in the Overleaf editor. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data we access
The extension reads keystrokes and editor content locally in your browser solely to detect and expand LaTeX shortcuts in real time. This processing happens entirely on your device.

## Data we collect
We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any user data. No information ever leaves your browser. The extension makes no network requests.

## Third parties
We do not share any data with third parties. No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.
